---
title: Blockchain & Smart Contract Development Course
---

The young blockchain and cryptocurrency industry has rapidly carved out its place in
the global economy and the world of information technology. Companies' growing interest
in building blockchain projects has made **"blockchain developer"** one of the most
in-demand roles in tech — with an average annual income roughly 40% higher than that of
other developers.

The Coiniran smart-contract team set out to help Persian-speaking developers gain the
technical knowledge and hands-on skills needed to build decentralized applications, by
running a free, public course on smart-contract programming with Solidity.
